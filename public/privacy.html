1. About SafarOne

SafarOne is a Muslim travel-planning application developed by Firas Badran. Support contact: planetearthkh@gmail.com. Last updated: July 2, 2026.

2. Information stored locally

Depending on your choices, your browser may store app language and preferences, saved itineraries, prepared offline flight plans, optional travel details, flights, accommodation, reservations, provider contacts, booking references you enter, cached exchange-rate or weather data where implemented, app-shell files, and service-worker files.

  • Browser local storage is not end-to-end encrypted.
  • Other people with access to the same device or browser profile may be able to access locally stored information.
  • Clearing browser or site data, uninstalling the PWA, browser cleanup, or private-browsing behavior may remove stored information.
  • Do not enter passport numbers, identity-document numbers, payment-card details, CVV, banking passwords, or account passwords.
  • Booking references are excluded from SafarOne share text and calendar exports by the app’s current design, but you should review content before sharing.

3. Location information

Location access is optional and requires browser or device permission. It may be used for Qibla direction and nearby-place searches. SafarOne does not create a user account or maintain its own remote location-history database. When you request live map, nearby-place, weather, routing, or related data, coordinates or search terms may be sent to the relevant third-party provider to fulfill the request. You can deny location permission and use destination or manual-search features where available.

4. Third-party services and external links

SafarOne uses service categories that may include OpenStreetMap and Overpass-related place/map data, OpenFreeMap map tiles and styles, Open-Meteo weather, Frankfurter exchange-rate data, Wikimedia Commons, Wikipedia and Wikidata for attraction information and media, GitHub/GitHub Pages for hosting and optional app-problem reports, Apple Maps or other external map links you select, and browser/device services such as geolocation, Web Share, clipboard, printing, calendar-file downloads, and service workers.

These services may receive standard technical request information such as IP address, browser headers, requested URL, search terms, destination, or coordinates necessary to provide the requested service. Their own privacy policies apply. SafarOne does not add third-party tracking scripts, remote fonts, analytics pixels, cookie banners, or advertising.

5. Support email

When you email planetearthkh@gmail.com, you choose what information to send. Email providers process messages under their own terms and privacy practices. Do not email passport, identity-document, payment-card, password, or highly sensitive travel information. Support emails may be retained as reasonably needed to respond, troubleshoot, prevent abuse, and maintain support records. You may request deletion of support correspondence by emailing the same address, subject to legitimate security or legal retention needs.

6. Reports and corrections

Report a problem can open OpenStreetMap or a prefilled GitHub issue. Opening an external page does not submit automatically. You control whether to submit externally. Report text should exclude saved travel details and booking references. GitHub or OpenStreetMap account requirements and privacy rules may apply.

7. Sharing and exports

Share Trip, Copy Itinerary, calendar export, and Print / Save PDF are initiated by you. These outputs may include itinerary and non-private travel-detail information. Booking references are excluded by the app’s current design. Once information is shared, copied, printed, downloaded, or passed to another app, SafarOne cannot control how the recipient or other app handles it. Review output before sharing.

8. Children

SafarOne is a general travel-planning tool and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children through an account system. The app does not offer user accounts. A parent or guardian should supervise a child’s use and prevent entry of sensitive personal information.

9. Security

Local-only storage reduces some data-transfer risks, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. Protect your device, browser profile, and downloaded or shared files.

10. User choices and deletion

You can deny or revoke location permission through browser or device settings, delete individual saved trips, delete travel details, clear the site’s browser storage/cache, remove an installed web app/PWA, or contact support. Clearing storage is destructive and may remove all locally saved trips.

11. Changes to this policy

The last-updated date will be updated when material changes are made.
